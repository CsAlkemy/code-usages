Your Claude limits,
always in the menu bar

A tiny macOS app that shows your claude.ai current-session and weekly usage as a live progress ring — the numbers you'd otherwise dig out of Settings, one glance away. Sign in once; everything stays on your Mac.

  • ●Session-first. The ring tracks your 5-hour session limit — the one that actually interrupts you — with weekly limits a click away.
  • ●Native glass. The popover uses real macOS vibrancy, light and dark.
  • ●Private by design. Reads your own logged-in session locally. No account, no API key, nothing leaves your machine.
  • ●Featherweight. Built with Tauri — the whole app is about a 3 MB download.
